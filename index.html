Get to know more about Hair Day

The #1 Hair Salon in Madison

Located in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is our exclusive boutique hair salon renowned for our expertise in cut, color, and styling. Beyond hair services, we also offer makeup and nail treatments in an ambiance that is both inviting and serene. Our salon has garnered a reputation for our avant-garde hair colors and up-to-the-minute haircut styles.

Due to our popularity, we operate on a limited coloring appointment schedule each week, necessitating our clients to make reservations well in advance. To keep our clients engaged and inspired between visits, our team regularly shares makeup and nail tutorials on our website.

Pria, our visionary owner, is a certified colorist who swiftly immersed herself in pioneering custom coloring techniques. Her vision was to craft an indulgent "treat yourself" haven for our clientele. Collaborating with her accomplished friend and hair maestro, Garry, we have assembled a talented team comprising two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one makeup artist, totaling seven skilled professionals.
